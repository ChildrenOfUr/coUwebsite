FAQ

  • Oh no! There's things missing from the game!

    Yep! There's lot of things that haven't made it into the game yet. Don't worry! We're working on it, promise. If you think that you should be seeing the feature and are not, check our Features page to see what's made it into the game so far. If the feature is missing when you play, go ahead and send us a bug report! We'd love to know.

  • I can't find the chat panel.

    If you are using a mobile device to access the game, then the chat panel is hidden by default, in order to show more of the game while you play. In the upper-right panel of the screen

  • Why aren't you working with the other groups to make the game?

    Great question! Our team started working on this game before Tiny Speck released their assets to the public. We made some good progress before a lot of the other teams were formed. We comlpetely support the other groups' work, we just have different skills and plans for the future of Glitch. It's like comparing apples to mangosteens.

Can't find an answer to your question? Head on over to our blog and give us a shout out!

Chat Commands

You can use these commands to change things in game. To use these commands, click on the chat box in the Global Chat pane, type in the command, and press the Enter key.

Command Description
/setname your_name This will change your avatar's name. Ex. /setname stoot barfield
/go tsid This will teleport you to a street of your choice. Look up the desired Location and copy the 15-character code at the end of the URL in the address bar. Use this as the tsid. Ex. For Fern End, the tsid is LCRTTKP69DO1MG0. Enter /go LCRTTKP69DO1MG0
